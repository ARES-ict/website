Introduction: Screen Time & Gadgets

This is a student-created website that transforms research findings into relatable, real-life school scenarios. It explores the positive effects of limiting screen time on our overall well-being. By consciously reducing exposure to digital devices, we can improve focus, enhance sleep quality, boost mental clarity, and foster stronger interpersonal connections.
